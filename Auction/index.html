AM

We started off the week by working on our projects. On Tuesday, Tyler Maniez came and gave us advice and tips for our Resumes, internships and interviews that we just recently applied for. The next day Carol Spradling from Northwest Missouri State University visited our class. While she was here we showed her our projects and she offered advice. On Thursday Brant Linch, another guest speaker, came and talked to our class. At the end of the week, we had visitors from Waynesville School District as well as some students attending the NKC and MCC Career Education fair.



PM

On Monday we talked about what the week was going to look like and we discussed our internships. We also continued to work on our projects and really hammer the nail on the head. On Tuesday we had a guest speaker Tyler Maniez from Cerner who was in HR. He discussed many topics such as interviews, internships, and resumes with us. We continued our projects the rest of the day. We continued to work on projects Wednesday-- as the semester is winding down we must finish up our projects before we are sent off to our internships. We had more project time for the class! Friday we are going to have visitors from Waynesville R-VI School Distract. We will have time after we answer questions to continue with our project work.
